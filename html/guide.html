How to Play: ZH2D

Version 1.0: Zombie Hunters 2D

Controls:

Use WASD to move.

Use Q to switch between guns (Shotgun and Revolver).

Use E pick up ammo.

Use SPACE to use a knife.

Basics:

SURVIVE!!

Press E on the gray circles to refill your revolver and shotgun.

The closer you are to your enemy the more damage your shotgun does.

Your knife does no damage unless the zombie is in the circle.
